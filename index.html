About RMC Kodai House

RMC Kodai House is launched in the Princess of Hills for our prestigious guests to enjoy the beauty of God's nature, cold climate and the picturesque destination

Our Kodai House is situated in the right location in Naidupuram which is only about 4kms from Kodaikanal Lake. The rooms are built to view the picturesque hills as it is located on one of the highest peaks. We have rooms with balconies too .

We provide services such as Homely Food at very affordable cost, Good Kitchen Facilities, Car Parking. Seperate room for Drivers, Camp Fire, Barbeque and Room Service We know the importance of Holidays. Keeping this in mind we have built our Kodai House to provide peace and tranquility for our guests to take great memories back home.

The modern facilities at Kodai House will make you enjoy the enchantment of Kodai. The friendly staff will strive to make sure your stay is safe and pleasant, so that you can rediscover the beauty of Kodaikanal.
